about us

The Official Astronomy Club Of Manipal

Empowering curiosity and fostering a passion for the cosmos, our astronomy club at Manipal strives to create an inclusive space for students to explore the wonders of the universe. Through stargazing events, educational outreach, and collaborative projects, we aim to inspire a deeper appreciation for astronomy and cultivate a community that celebrates the beauty of the night sky. Join us on a journey of discovery as we explore the mysteries of the cosmos together.

Events

Explore a cosmic blend of knowledge and excitement at our Astronomy Club events. From captivating stargazing nights to informative workshops and celestial competitions, join us for stellar experiences that bring the wonders of the universe closer to home.

Star Gazing Nights

Equipped with telescopes, a will to explore and guided by experienced enthusiasts, participants immerse themselves in the beauty of distant galaxies, sparkling constellations, and the mesmerizing glow of planets.

Seminar with the experts

Renowned astronomers share their latest research and insights into various celestial phenomena. Attendees have the opportunity to interact with experts, and delve into topics ranging from black holes to the origins of the universe.

Planetarium Visits

Visiting the planetarium is always an enriching experience for our club members. Stepping into the immersive environment of the dome-shaped theater, we are transported to distant galaxies and cosmic wonders.

Our Projects

Astronomy projects encompass observations, research, outreach, collaboration,
astrophotography, workshops, publications, competitions,
and community science.

Research and paper review


Our esteemed astronomy club provides unparalleled opportunities for scholarly research, meticulous paper reviews, and in-depth celestial exploration for interested enthusiasts.


Telescope mount


Building the mount was an exciting task. From selecting sturdy materials to assembling components, each step contributes to the stability and performance of a telescope.


Star tracking API


Developing a star tracking API requires designing a user-friendly interface, integrating with star databases, and ensuring accurate celestial positioning information.
